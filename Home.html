    Introduction of
    Computer







    • A computer is an electronic device that processes data according to instructions provided by computer programs.
      It can store, retrieve, and process data using a wide range of tasks. The term "computer" can refer to a single machine or a group of interconnected computers, such as a computer network or cluster. A computer system typically consists of a
      central processing unit (CPU), memory, input devices (like keyboards and mice), output devices (like monitors and printers), and peripheral devices that perform both input and output functions, such as touchscreens. Computers are used in various applications,
      from personal computing to scientific research, and have significantly impacted the way we store, process, and share information.
